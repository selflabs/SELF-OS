# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We aim for a respectful, professional environment. Harassment, discrimination, and abusive behavior are not acceptable.

## Reporting

Concerns may be reported to repository maintainers through the contact channels they provide for this project.

This code of conduct is adapted from common open-source community norms; projects may replace it with the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) or another published policy as maintainers prefer.
